ABOUT

This is an initiative to make students aware of Drupal, and to explore and be in touch with the community by contributing to the Drupal Core. The students would get exposed to basic functionalities like Issue Queues, the Drupal Ladder and some of the Core projects to work on to learn more about Drupal and to start their contribution towards this exciting world of Content Management.
