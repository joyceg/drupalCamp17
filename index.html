Drupal Camp Amrita 2017

This is an initiative to help students contribute to Drupal core projects, make them aware about the opportunities to explore and be in touch with the community. The students are exposed to the basic practises like issue queues, Drupal ladder and some of the projects to work on to learn more about Drupal and to start their contributions towards this exciting world of content management system.
